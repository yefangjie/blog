## 1. AI 范式革命 | AI Paradigm Revolution

### 中文
2026年的AI行业正在经历一场悄无声息的范式革命。当所有人的目光还聚焦大语言模型的军备竞赛时，真正的变革却在世界模型领域悄然发生。

大语言模型（LLM）虽然在自然语言处理上取得了惊人成就，但其本质仍是统计模式匹配，缺乏对世界的真正理解。而世界模型（World Model）则试图构建一个能够模拟真实世界、具备因果推理能力的AI系统。

### English
The AI industry in 2026 is experiencing a quiet paradigm revolution. While everyone's attention focuses on the LLM arms race, the real transformation is happening in World Models.

Large Language Models, despite their impressive NLP achievements, remain essentially statistical pattern matching without true world understanding. World Models aim to build AI systems that can simulate the real world and perform causal reasoning.

## 4. 世界模型 vs 大语言模型 | World Models vs LLMs

### 中文
**世界模型（World Model）**是AMI Labs的核心理念：

大语言模型 vs 世界模型：
- 学习方式：统计模式匹配 vs 模拟世界运行
- 推理能力：表面相关 vs 因果推理
- 泛化能力：受限于训练数据 vs 跨领域迁移
- 对世界的理解：文本统计 vs 物理规律

Yann LeCun直言："语言是毒药"。他认为，语言只是人类智能的表层，真正的智能需要理解世界的物理规律。

### English
**World Models** are AMI Labs' core philosophy:

LLMs vs World Models:
- Learning: Statistical pattern matching vs World simulation
- Reasoning: Surface correlations vs Causal inference
- Generalization: Training data limited vs Cross-domain transfer
- World understanding: Text statistics vs Physical laws

Yann LeCun famously said: "Language is poison." Language is just the surface of human intelligence; true intelligence requires understanding physical world laws.
