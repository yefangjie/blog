Overview

OpenAI’s Strategic Pivot

  • Enterprise partnerships deepening

Shopify Agents

Shopify’s approach to AI agents in e-commerce:

  • Merchant assistance for store management
  • Customer interaction automation
  • Inventory optimization through AI

Market Trends
