## Overview

Persistent security challenges facing IoT devices and why the problem continues.

## The Core Problem

### A History of Insecurity
- IoT security has been a concern "forever"
- Devices continue shipping with inadequate security
- Consumer-grade security in enterprise deployments

### Root Causes
- **Cost pressure** drives security to the bottom
- **Speed to market** trumps security review
- **No secure-by-design** approach

## Notable Incidents

- **Mirai Botnet (2016)**: 600,000+ compromised devices
- **Ring Camera Hacks (2019)**: Unauthorized home access
- **Smart Home Data Leaks**: Unencrypted transmission

## Solutions

- **Secure Boot**: Verify firmware integrity
- **Network Segmentation**: Isolate IoT devices
- **Mandatory Updates**: Legal requirements for support
