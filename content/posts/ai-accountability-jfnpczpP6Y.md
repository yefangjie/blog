## Overview

Examining the complex question of accountability in AI agent systems.

## The Accountability Problem

Why it's complex:
- **Autonomous decision-making** by AI systems
- **Multiple stakeholders** in agent ecosystems
- **Opaque reasoning** in AI decision processes

## Stakeholder Perspectives

### End Users
Responsible for intent and oversight, must understand agent capabilities.

### AI Developers
Responsible for design, safety, guardrails, and testing.

### Platform Providers
Responsible for infrastructure, access controls, and monitoring.

## Key Takeaways

- Accountability is **distributed**
- Context matters for different scenarios
- Legal frameworks are still evolving
- Human oversight remains crucial
